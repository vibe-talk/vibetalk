Resources & External Links

Curated collection of authoritative resources for online safety, mental health support, and further reading.

This page collects trusted external resources that supplement our guides. These organizations and publications provide authoritative information on online safety, mental health, research, and support services.

Mental Health Support

If you're struggling with mental health issues, these organizations offer professional support and resources.

Global Resources

  • World Health Organization - Mental Health

    Global information on mental health conditions, treatments, and initiatives. Authoritative resource for understanding mental health worldwide.

  • NAMI - National Alliance on Mental Illness

    The largest grassroots mental health organization in the US, providing education, support groups, and advocacy.

  • MentalHealth.gov

    US government resource providing information on mental health conditions, treatment locators, and how to help someone in crisis.

  • Find A Helpline

    Global directory of mental health helplines. Enter your country to find relevant crisis support numbers.

India-Specific

  • NIMHANS

    National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences, India's premier mental health institution offering information and treatment services.

  • iCall

    Psychosocial helpline by Tata Institute of Social Sciences offering free telephone counselling. Call: 9152987821

  • Vandrevala Foundation

    24/7 free mental health helpline for India. Call: 1860-2662-345

In Crisis? If you're in immediate danger or having thoughts of self-harm, please contact emergency services in your country immediately. In India, call 112 or your local emergency number.

Online Safety Organizations

Organizations dedicated to promoting internet safety, preventing cybercrime, and educating users.

  • StaySafeOnline.org (National Cybersecurity Alliance)

    Comprehensive resource for online safety education, including guides on protecting privacy, recognizing phishing, and secure practices.

  • ConnectSafely

    Nonprofit dedicated to educating users about safety, privacy, and security on social media and the internet.

  • National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (India)

    Official Indian government portal for reporting cybercrime including harassment, fraud, and illegal content. Helpline: 1930

  • Internet Matters

    UK-based organization providing resources for online safety, particularly focused on protecting children and young people.

  • CERT-In (Indian Computer Emergency Response Team)

    Government organization handling cybersecurity incidents and providing security advisories for India.

Research & Publications

Academic and scientific resources for those interested in the psychology of social connection and online communication.

  • American Psychological Association (APA)

    Professional organization publishing research on psychology topics including social connection, loneliness, and online behavior.

  • Pew Research Center - Internet & Technology

    Nonpartisan research on internet usage trends, social media impact, and digital life. Excellent source for statistics and studies.

  • Google Scholar

    Search engine for scholarly literature. Search terms like "social connection" "anonymity online" or "stranger conversation psychology" for academic papers.

  • PubMed

    Database of biomedical and life sciences literature, including mental health and psychology research.

Privacy Tools

Tools and services that help protect your privacy online.

  • PrivacyTools.io

    Community resource recommending privacy-focused software and services, including VPNs, browsers, and messaging apps.

  • EFF Tools

    Electronic Frontier Foundation's collection of privacy and security tools, including HTTPS Everywhere and Privacy Badger.

  • ProtonVPN

    Privacy-focused VPN service with a free tier. Based in Switzerland, known for strong privacy protections.

  • Mullvad VPN

    Privacy-focused VPN that doesn't require personal information to sign up. Known for strong security practices.

Digital Wellbeing

Resources for maintaining a healthy relationship with technology.

  • Center for Humane Technology

    Organization working to realign technology with humanity's best interests, including resources on mindful tech use.

  • Google Digital Wellbeing

    Resources and tools for managing screen time and creating healthy digital habits.

  • Common Sense Media

    Reviews, ratings, and resources helping families navigate the digital world safely and responsibly.

Further Reading

Books and articles for deeper exploration of topics covered in our guides.

  • "The Power of Strangers" by Joe Keohane

    Explores why we should talk to strangers and the surprising benefits of connecting with people we don't know.

  • "Reclaiming Conversation" by Sherry Turkle

    MIT professor examines how we've sacrificed conversation for mere connection, and how to reclaim it.

  • "Alone Together" by Sherry Turkle

    Explores the complex relationship between humans and technology in the digital age.

  • TED Talks: Communication

    Collection of TED talks on communication, conversation, and connecting with others.
